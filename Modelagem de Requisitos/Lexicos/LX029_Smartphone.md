## Título: Smartphone

## Classificação:

- Objeto

## Noção:

- Aparelho de telefone celular provido de sistema operacional e acesso á internet.
- Objeto que contêm o [Aplicativo](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX002_Aplicativo.md) [Ribon](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX026_Ribon.md)
- Sistema pelo qual o [Usuário](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX031_Usuário.md) interage com o [Aplicativo](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX002_Aplicativo.md)

## Impacto:


- O [Usuário](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX031_Usuário.md) instala o [Aplicativo](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX002_Aplicativo.md) [Ribon](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX026_Ribon.md)
- O [Usuário](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX031_Usuário.md) doa [Ribons](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX026_Ribon.md) pelo smartphone
- O [Usuário](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX031_Usuário.md) lê as [História](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX014_Historia.md)s pelo smartphone
- O [Usuário](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX031_Usuário.md) compra [Ribons](https://github.com/requisitos-2019-1/Ribon/blob/master/Modelagem%20de%20Requisitos/Lexicos/LX026_Ribon.md) pelo smartphone
